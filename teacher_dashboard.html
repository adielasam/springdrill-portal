Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Springdrill portal. To deploy an exam:

  1. Go to Create New Test to define the subject, class, and time limits.
  2. Navigate to Manage CBT to upload your questions via Word Document (.docx) and click "Publish to Class Dashboard".
  3. Direct your students to log in and navigate to their Available Tests section.
